# PES Tamilans Auction Management System

# Business Rules

Version: 1.0

---

## BR-001 - Retention Price

A retained player's price is exactly equal to the previous tournament's winning bid.

Status: Locked

---

## BR-002 - Maximum Retention

Each team can retain only one player.

Status: Locked

---

## BR-003 - RTM

Each team can use RTM only once.

Status: Locked

---

## BR-004 - One Auction

Each tournament contains exactly one auction.

Status: Locked

---

## BR-005 - Audience

Audience users do not require authentication.

Status: Locked

---

## BR-006 - Team Budget

Initial Budget = Tournament Budget

Remaining Budget = Initial Budget − Retention Cost − Auction Spending

Status: Locked

---

## BR-007 - Team Assignment

Captains can be assigned to different teams in different tournaments.

Status: Locked

---

## BR-008 - Players

Every tournament has its own player list.

Status: Locked

---

## BR-009 - Teams

Every tournament has its own teams.

Status: Locked
